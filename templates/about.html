About Our Ministry

Empowering Youth and Promoting Sports Excellence in Nasarawa State

Our History

The Ministry of Youth and Sports Development came into existence in the year 1996, following the creation of Nasarawa State out of Plateau State by the administration of Late Gen. Sani Abacha. It later metamorphosed into the Ministry of Youth and Sports Development in the year 2000.

The Ministry was recreated in September, 2003, by His Excellency, Alhaji (Dr.) Abdullahi Adamu, with the major aim and objective of developing Youth and Sports in all aspects through various youth and sports activities.

Our Vision

  • To develop a youth base system aimed at empowering and projecting the talents and sporting potentials that abound in the state.
  • To create an enabling environment for peaceful co-existence among the youth and people of different ethno-religious strata.
  • To portray the sporting potentials of Nasarawa State to the world.

Our Mission

  • To discover hidden talents that is spread across the state for podium heights.
  • To use sport as a means of revenue generation and empowerment of the youths in the state.
  • To participate in local, national and international sporting programs.

Our Core Values

Peaceful Coexistence

Creating a peaceful atmosphere for people of different values, faith and beliefs.

Youth Employment

Creation of jobs for the unemployed youths.

Health & Wellness

Enhancing the health factor of an individual.

Social Cohesion

Breaking barriers and stereotype through social cohesion.

Performance Enhancement

To boost the confidence of an individual and enhance performance.

Community Development

Project the image of an individual and his community on value reorientation.
